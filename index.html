About Geelong

Located 75 kilometers southwest of Melbourne, Geelong is a vibrant coastal city known for its stunning waterfront, thriving arts scene, excellent schools, and family-friendly atmosphere. Geelong offers the perfect balance of urban convenience and relaxed coastal living.

Why People Love Geelong

  • Scenic waterfront views and easy access to the iconic Great Ocean Road.
  • A lively arts and culture scene, including the Geelong Gallery and Arts Centre.
  • Top schools like Geelong Grammar School and The Geelong College.
  • Outdoor lifestyle with parks like the Geelong Botanic Gardens and Eastern Park.

Education and Amenities

Geelong is home to some of Victoria's best schools and universities, including Deakin University. The city also boasts a wide range of recreational facilities, including sports clubs, fitness centers, and community hubs, making it a great place for all ages.

Nearby Suburbs

The surrounding suburbs add to the appeal of Geelong, offering even more options for living, education, and recreation:

  • Belmont: A popular family suburb with great parks and excellent schools.
  • Newtown: Known for its heritage homes, cafes, and proximity to the city center.
  • Highton: Features large homes, scenic views, and close access to the Barwon River.
  • Grovedale: A family-friendly suburb with easy access to the city and great schools.
